IVFToday

Daily IVF instructions, changes, and low-stock alerts.

IVFToday is a local-first iPhone app that helps patients organize IVF treatment instructions from screenshots and PDFs without sending sensitive data to a backend.

Support Privacy

Import instructions locally

Bring in screenshots, photos, or PDFs, review extracted text, and confirm what should be applied before it affects your daily plan.

See what changed

Compare the latest instructions with previous ones so medication, appointment, and timing changes are easier to catch.

Track today's critical tasks

Keep medications and appointments in one place, with higher emphasis on time-sensitive or higher-risk steps.

Stay ahead of low inventory

Estimate days left for medication on hand and warn before a shortage interrupts critical doses.

Built for privacy

  • On-device OCR and PDF text extraction
  • No account required
  • No cloud sync in the current release

IVFToday is an organizational support tool and does not replace direct clinic instructions or medical advice.
