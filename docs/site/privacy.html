Privacy

IVFToday Privacy Summary

IVFToday is designed as a local-first iPhone app. In the current release, core workflow data stays on the user's device.

What the app stores

  • Medication plans and schedules
  • Appointments and treatment workflow history
  • Inventory counts and notification preferences
  • Imported text derived from screenshots or PDFs you choose to process

What the app does not require

  • No account creation
  • No backend login
  • No cloud sync in the current release

On-device processing

Image OCR and PDF text extraction are intended to run locally on device. Imported content should be reviewed by the user before it is applied to any treatment plan.

Your responsibility

Users should verify all doses, timing, and appointments against direct clinic instructions. IVFToday is not medical advice and does not replace clinical guidance.
